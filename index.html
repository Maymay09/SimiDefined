Our Story

Born From Passion

SIMI DEFINED was born from a belief that Nigerian women deserve fashion that celebrates their heritage while elevating their presence in every room they walk into.

Founded in Lagos, we blend the richness of African textile tradition with modern silhouettes, creating pieces that speak confidence, luxury, and identity. Every garment is made with intention — for the woman who refuses to be ordinary.
